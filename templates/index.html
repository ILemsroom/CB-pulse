Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What equipment do I need?
A computer with a stable internet connection, a working camera, and a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge). No additional software installation required.
Can I pause the exam once started?
No. Once started, the timer runs continuously. Ensure you have uninterrupted time before beginning. Emergency pauses may be granted by a proctor in exceptional circumstances.
How are results delivered?
Instant preliminary score upon submission. Official results with detailed analytics and rationales are available immediately via your dashboard.
What if I lose connection during an exam?
Your session is preserved. You have 5 minutes to reconnect before auto-submission. All answers up to the disconnection point are saved.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. You can attempt one partial mock exam (20 questions) for free to evaluate the platform before purchasing any exam pack.
